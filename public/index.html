Frequently Asked Questions

Do you store my images after processing?

No. All files are processed securely in memory or temporarily stored on our server only for processing. They are automatically deleted immediately after completion.

Are your tools free to use?

Yes, our tools are 100% free with no sign-up, no watermarks, and no limits!

Which file formats are supported?

We currently support .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp, .tiff, .bmp, and .avif formats.

Can I resize images for social media?

Yes! Our image resizer includes presets for popular platforms like Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and YouTube — in both portrait, landscape, and square formats.

Can I convert images between formats?

Absolutely. You can convert between JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, BMP, and TIFF formats directly in your browser.

Is there a limit to how many images I can process?

Currently, you can process up to 10 images at a time for each operation to ensure smooth performance.

Do you support large files?

Yes. You can upload files up to 200MB each, and our backend will handle them efficiently.

Will the quality of my image drop when I compress it?

You choose the quality level before compression. Higher quality means a larger file size, while lower quality means more compression.

Do you support TIFF images?

Yes. We process TIFF images and also provide a preview placeholder if your browser cannot display them.

Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Yes. Our site is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop browsers.

Do I need to install anything?

No downloads or installations are required — all tools run directly in your browser.
